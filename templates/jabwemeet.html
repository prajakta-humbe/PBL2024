Jab We Met Travel Itinerary

  • Mumbai

    Start your journey in the vibrant city of Mumbai, where the lively spirit of Geet and the serendipitous encounters begin.

  • Rohtang Pass

    Embark on an adventurous journey to Rohtang Pass, a place where Aditya and Geet's destinies intertwine amidst the breathtaking beauty of the mountains.

  • Manali

    Experience the charm of Manali, where Aditya and Geet's journey unfolds, filled with laughter, love, and unexpected twists.

  • Bhatinda

    Visit Bhatinda, Geet's hometown, and immerse yourself in the colorful and vibrant Punjabi culture.

  • Shimla

    Explore the picturesque hill station of Shimla, where Aditya and Geet's journey takes unexpected turns, leading to self-discovery and love.
