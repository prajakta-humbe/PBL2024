Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani Travel Itinerary

  • Manali

    Begin your adventure in Manali, where Naina, Bunny, Aditi, and Avi's journey of friendship and love begins amidst the snow-capped mountains.

  • Paris

    Experience the magic of Paris, where Bunny pursues his dreams and Naina learns to embrace life to the fullest amidst the iconic landmarks of the city of love.

  • Kasol

    Discover the tranquility of Kasol, where Naina and Bunny find solace and understanding in each other's company amidst the serene beauty of nature.

  • Kolkata

    Experience the vibrancy of Kolkata, where Naina, Bunny, Aditi, and Avi reunite to celebrate life, friendship, and new beginnings.
