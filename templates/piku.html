Piku Travel Itinerary

  • Kolkata

    Begin your journey in the bustling city of Kolkata, where Piku's story unfolds amidst the chaos of everyday life.

  • Delhi

    Explore the historic city of Delhi, where Piku's journey to fulfill her father's last wish begins.

  • Kolkata to Varanasi

    Travel from Kolkata to Varanasi, experiencing the emotional rollercoaster of Piku, Rana, and Bhaskor's road trip.

  • Varanasi

    Discover the spiritual city of Varanasi, where Piku confronts her fears and finds closure amidst the sacred Ganges.
